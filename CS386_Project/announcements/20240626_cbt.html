Console Closed Beta Test Details

June 26, 2024

1. Test Period

The Closed Beta Test kicks off July 23rd at 22:00 UTC (3:00 PM PDT, 6:00 PM EDT, July 24th at 7:00 UTC+9) and continues until August 5th at 7:00 UTC (12:00 AM PDT, 3:00 AM EDT, 16:00 UTC+9).

2. How to Join

On Console

  • Complete the questionnaire at: https://research.easebar.com/htmls/dwhgcy/paper.html
  • Keep an eye on your email to see if you get selected and stay tuned to our socials and Discord for updates.

On Steam

  • Visit the Marvel Rivals Steam store page starting at 22:00 UTC on July 20th
  • Click "Request Access" under "Join Marvel Rivals Playtest," to sign up

As a token of appreciation for our dedicated players, we will start confirming access daily after the sign-ups go live. Those selected will be promptly notified.

Additional content, including events, tournaments, and live streams, will be unveiled in July. Following the positive response to our Extra Invite Codes event during the Alpha test, we have similar plans for this CBT, expanding access to Marvel Rivals to more players. Stay tuned for updates.
