MARVEL RIVALS CLOSED ALPHA TEST BEGINS IN MAY!

March 28, 2024

Marvel Rivals is opening a Closed Alpha Test starting May. In this Alpha Test, players will have access to more than a dozen playable Super Heroes and Super Villains, including iconic characters such as Black Panther, Spider-Man, Magneto, and Scarlet Witch!

SIGN UP FOR THE CLOSED ALPHA

Stay tuned for further updates regarding the exact start date and other details of the Closed Alpha Test. This is your chance to get an early taste of Marvel Rivals and contribute to its development.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I play the Closed Alpha?

The Closed Alpha Test will be available on PC.

What happens after I sign up for the Closed Alpha Test?

If you are selected to be a part of the Closed Alpha Test, then you can expect an email from us (please check your spam/junk folders as well) detailing all the next steps and access to Marvel Rivals.

What can I expect from the Closed Alpha Test?

During the Closed Alpha Test for Marvel Rivals, which is set to take place in May, participants can look forward to experiencing an early version of the game that is still in development. Prepare to test out some of the different Team-Up Skills and use the destructible environment to your advantage in ever-shifting battles. The primary focus of the Closed Alpha Test will be to test the game's mechanics, identify and resolve any bugs or issues, and gather valuable feedback from you.
